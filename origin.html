ORIGIN

Atoz is from a small island in the Mediterranean called "Passto". Where they eat pizza 3 times a day for 365 days. Everything is very subtle on the island, people hate being on a spotlight and people wear the same design outfit that circulates every 4 years. Even soccer slogan on the island is “Low Risk Play.”
